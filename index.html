Concise News

I built an app called Concise because I wanted to make staying informed feel a little less overwhelming. With Concise, I organized the news into categories, so you can easily find the topics that interest you most. Every evening, the app updates with the top 10 stories in each category, giving you the freshest news in a simple, straightforward way. No endless scrolling, just the most important headlines, neatly packaged to save you time and keep you up to date.

It’s designed to make campus life more interactive and ensure everyone feels involved, both during their time at university and beyond.
